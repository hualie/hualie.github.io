Biography

Hualie Jiang is currently a Ph.D. Candidate at CUHKSZ advised by Prof. Rui Huang. In the second half of 2020, he interned at Alibaba Cloud A.I. Labs, mentored by Dr. Siyu Zhu and Dr. Zilong Dong. He obtained an M.S. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2017 by spending one-year taking courses at campus and two years on research advised by Prof. Zhan Song at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received a B.S. degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2014.
