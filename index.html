I am a postdoctoral fellow at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I finished the Ph.D. at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) on 2021. I am working on monocular depth estimation, panoramic perception, and computer vision, etc.
