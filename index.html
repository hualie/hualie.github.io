Hualie Jiang


Hualie is currently a senior engineer at Insta360, working on monocular/binocular depth estimation, panoramic perception, and etc. He obtained my PhD degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) in 2021. He once had interned at Alibaba Cloud AI Lab and worked at Sensetime.
